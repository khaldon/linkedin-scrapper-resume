# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to a positive, inclusive, and supportive environment for all.

## Our Standards

- **Be respectful** – Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- **Be considerate** – Assume best intentions. Give the benefit of the doubt.
- **Be collaborative** – Share knowledge and help others.
- **Be constructive** – Provide feedback in a helpful, non‑personal manner.
- **Be mindful** – Avoid harassment, intimidation, or exclusion.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind, including but not limited to:
- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or any other protected characteristic.
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Threats, intimidation, or violent language.
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following.
- Publishing private or personal information without consent.
- Any other conduct that creates a hostile environment.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **contact@yourproject.org**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality and may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal from the project.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, etc.) and in external communications (social media, conferences, etc.) when they are related to the project.
